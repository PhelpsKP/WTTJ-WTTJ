THIS IS THE BATTLE OF OHIO PODCAST!

You know what is the most obnoxioius thing on the planet to me? Sports hosts and journalists who try to pretend like they're not a 'fan' of any NFL team. Allegedly, they're completely neutral parties with allegiences to only the FACTS. Okay.

In 2019 I started the Battle of Ohio Podcast with Thomas Stargell, a friend of mine from college and a rabid Browns fan. We're both football nerds and wanna-be analysts. We're not going to pretend to be neutral. Objective? Sure. Rational? Sometimes. That said, we're fans of our favorite teams and that's what this show is about.

This show is chalk full of rivalry smack talk. We love having opposing fans on with us and all of the interaction and smack-talk you guys bring! Join us every week on my Youtube channel to become part of the insanity.
